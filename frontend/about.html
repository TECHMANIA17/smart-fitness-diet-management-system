About SmartFit

A guided fitness management system for users and fitness admins.

SmartFit is designed around communication: users keep their profile and progress updated, while admins use that information to assign better diet plans, workout plans, and tips.

Create User Account User Login

The SmartFit Workflow

The app connects profile details, assigned guidance, daily progress, and admin follow-up into a simple cycle.

Step 1

User profile setup

A user enters body details, activity level, goal, diet preference, and workout preference so recommendations have useful context.

Step 2

Admin plan assignment

Admins can assign diet plans, workout plans, and tips to all users or to a specific user based on their profile.

Step 3

Progress review

Users record weight, calories, water, steps, workouts, and notes so progress can be reviewed clearly over time.

Purpose

Why the system is useful

Keeps fitness, diet, tips, BMI, and progress records in one organized place.
Reduces confusion between user activity and admin recommendations.
Helps users follow a routine and helps admins make better decisions.
Best Fit

Who can use SmartFit

Fitness centers that want a simple member management workflow.
Trainers who assign workout routines and review progress.
Diet consultants who provide structured meal guidance and habit tips.
Communication Model

How users and admins stay connected

Users do not need to search through unrelated information. Assigned plans and personal tips appear inside the user area, while admins can use the admin panel to update guidance whenever the user's profile or progress changes.

UserUpdates profile

Goal, diet preference, workout preference, and body details stay current.

AdminSends guidance

Plans and tips can be sent generally or to one specific user.

ProgressImproves follow-up

Daily entries help admins understand how the user is doing.

SmartFit supports healthier routines through organized planning, tracking, and admin-guided communication.
